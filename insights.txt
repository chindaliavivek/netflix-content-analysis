Netflix Content Analysis – Business Insights

1. Content Distribution
   -Movies dominate Netflix at ~70%.
   -TV Shows have risen rapidly since 2016.

2. Content Growth Trend
   -Sharp growth from 2015 to 2019.
   -2019 was the peak year for new titles added.

3. Country Insights
   -United States produces the highest number of titles.
   -India and UK are major contributors.
   -High international diversity in Netflix catalog.

4. Genre Analysis
   -Drama and Comedy are the most common genres.
   -Action & Thriller genres have grown in popularity.

5. Rating Distribution
   -TV-MA is the most frequent rating.
   -Netflix content leans toward mature audiences.

6. Duration Patterns
   -Movies mostly range between 80–120 mins.
   -TV Shows usually have 1–3 seasons.

Business Recommendations:
-Increase global content collaborations.
-Expand TV show production—strong growth segment.
-Enhance family-friendly content due to low representation.
